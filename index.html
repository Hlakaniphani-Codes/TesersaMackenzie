About Teresa Mackenzie

Our Expertise

Services You Can Rely On

Independent, impartial, and meticulously accurate — every assessment is backed by decades of experience.

Diamond Grading

Precise 4C evaluations — Cut, Color, Clarity, Carat — using GIA standards. Certified grading reports for insurance, resale, or personal knowledge.

Jewelry Appraisals

Comprehensive written appraisals for estate planning, insurance coverage, equitable distribution, and donation valuations. Fully documented and defensible.

Gemstone Dealing

Direct access to ethically sourced colored gemstones — sapphire, ruby, emerald, and more. Wholesale pricing for the trade and bespoke selection for collectors.

Trade Services

Bulk diamond grading, sourcing, and inventory valuation for retailers, auction houses, and estate liquidators. Confidential and efficient.

Insurance & Estate Reports

Detailed, photographically documented reports tailored for insurers, executors, and beneficiaries. Recognized by leading underwriters nationwide.

Our Collection

Exquisitely Crafted Jewelry

Each piece reflects timeless elegance, superior craftsmanship, and ethical sourcing.

Engagement Rings

Exquisitely crafted engagement rings featuring ethically sourced diamonds, timeless designs, and bespoke settings.

Each ring is handcrafted with precision, available in platinum, white gold, yellow gold, and rose gold. Our master jewelers ensure every detail reflects your unique love story. We also offer custom design services to bring your vision to life.

Necklaces

Elegant necklaces handcrafted with exceptional gemstones and precious metals, designed to illuminate your presence.

Our necklace collections feature sapphires, rubies, emeralds, and diamonds set in intricate designs. Whether a statement piece or a delicate chain, each necklace is crafted to enhance your natural beauty. Custom lengths and gemstone selections are available.

Bracelets

Graceful bracelets crafted in fine gold and platinum, adorned with brilliant gemstones, offering timeless elegance.

Our bracelets range from classic tennis bracelets to modern cuff designs, featuring diamonds, sapphires, and other precious stones. Each piece is designed for comfort and durability, perfect for daily wear or special occasions. Engraving services are also offered.
